Welcome to the College Feedback Chatbot website!

The requirement of reviews for the betterment of the colleges and universities
is often needed. Students often prevent giving feedbacks or reviews due to the
long and tedious forms to be filled or being uncomfortable while answering
questions. Students usually cannot express their opinions freely. They prefer a
feedback giving procedure that is less tedious and also anonymous. A software
that helps students freely express their opinions and easily, is often needed in
such situations.



Just deploy College Feedback Chatbot on educational institution website to collect
student's views and thoughts on their experience. Allow students to share how they
feel about their facility and staff. Understand factors that can help the institution
to improve. This interactive chatbot is designed to know how students feel about a
university's environment and facilities. If you are looking to know how you can
improve your students' satisfaction levels, try this today.
